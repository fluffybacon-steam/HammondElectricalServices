Meet Chuck Hammond,

Owner and operator of Hammond Electric. Chuck has been tinkering with electronics since he was 8 years old and has always had a gift for all things electrical. After serving two tours in Afghanistan as a Marine, Chuck decided to pursue his passion and become an electrician.

High Quality, Reliable Service

At Hammond Electric, Chuck prides himself on providing the highest quality work and ensuring complete customer satisfaction. He loves taking on challenging projects and is always up for a good electrical puzzle. With his years of experience and dedication to his craft, Chuck is the go-to electrician for all of your electrical needs. Contact us today to schedule an appointment.
