What services does Hammond Electric offer?

Hammond Electric offers a full range of residential, commercial, and indoor electrical services. You can find a detailed listing of our services here.

How long has Hammond Electric been in business?

Hammond Electric was founded by Chuck Hammond in 2015. Hammond Electric has making customers happy ever since.

Are the electricians at Hammond Electric licensed and insured?

Yes, all of the electricians at Hammond Electric are licensed and insured. Chuck Hammond holds a valid Home Improvement Contractor license (HIC: #PA133166), and all of our team members are trained and experienced in the latest electrical techniques and technologies.

How does Hammond Electric ensure customer satisfaction?

At Hammond Electric, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We pride ourselves on providing high-quality work and excellent customer service. We will take the time to listen to your needs and concerns, and will work closely with you to ensure that your project is completed to your satisfaction.

Can I schedule an appointment with Hammond Electric online?

Yes, you can easily schedule an appointment with Hammond Electric online. Simply visit our website and fill out the contact form, or give us a call and one of our team members will be happy to assist you. We offer flexible scheduling options to fit your needs and schedule.
